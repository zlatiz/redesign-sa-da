Structure

SA-DA Architecture is a Brooklyn-based practice dedicated to the precision of material and the poetry of space.

Our Philosophy

We believe that architecture is the silent choreography of light against mass.

Founded in the heart of Brooklyn, SA-DA approaches every project as a unique site-specific inquiry. We strip away the superfluous to find the essential structural truth of a building. Our work is defined by an uncompromising commitment to technical precision and a deep respect for the materials we use.

From private residences to commercial interventions, our methodology remains constant: we design from the inside out, ensuring that the 'void'—the lived space—is as carefully considered as the 'structure' that contains it.

Process

From Concept to Construction

01

Analysis

Deep dive into site conditions, historical context, and the fundamental needs of the inhabitant. We listen before we draw.

02

Synthesis

Translating raw data into spatial volume. This is where the dialogue between structure and void begins to take physical form.

03

Execution

Rigorous technical oversight. We collaborate with master craftsmen to ensure that the final build matches the initial vision with millimetric accuracy.
